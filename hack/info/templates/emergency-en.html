Emergency Guide for International Students

1. In Case of Medical Emergency

Dial 119 for an ambulance.

Go to the nearest hospital or clinic. You can find hospital locations through our app/website.

If you cannot speak Korean, use a translation app or show the following phrase:

I need medical help. Please take me to the hospital.
(Korean: 저는 병원이 필요합니다. 병원에 데려다 주세요.)

2. In Case of Fire

Dial 119 to report a fire.

Evacuate immediately and use the nearest emergency exit. Avoid using elevators. Use stairs.

If there is smoke, cover your nose and mouth with a cloth and stay low to the ground.

3. In Case of Natural Disaster (Earthquake, Typhoon)

Earthquake:

Take cover under a sturdy table or desk. Stay indoors until the shaking stops.

Typhoon:

Stay indoors and avoid going out unless necessary. Keep updated with weather alerts.

Dial 119 if you need emergency rescue assistance.

4. In Case of Crime or Dangerous Situation

Dial 112 to report a crime (the police).

If you feel unsafe, move to a crowded or public place.

Keep your belongings secure and avoid walking alone late at night.

5. Emergency Numbers and Useful Phrases

119: Fire Department / Ambulance

112: Police

Useful phrase: I am in an emergency. Please help.
(Korean: 긴급 상황입니다. 도와주세요.)
